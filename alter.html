Introduction

"Alter" is a mobile app for creatives looking to buy and sell customised clothing and accessories. Alter was created to address the team's frustrations with the user experience of mobile apps when attempting to easily buy and sell custom clothing, start their own small clothing business or expand their current ones.

This project was created as a hypothetical collaboration with "Grailed" to target a different customer segment that wants to buy and sell customised clothing and accessories.

Our team designed an easy-to-use mobile platform for clothing designers ages 17 to 50s and above.

My Role

For this project, I collaborated on user research, wireframing, low and high-fidelity mockups, user testing and front-end development.
